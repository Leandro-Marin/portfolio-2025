GREENBEAN

2022

Greenbean is a personal project that brings vegetarian cuisine into the digital age with a fresh and ironic twist. A recipe website where classic dishes get a modern, plant-based makeover—because eating green doesn’t have to be boring. I designed the full brand identity and the UX/UI for the site, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices. Plus, I built the website from scratch with HTML and CSS, stepping into the front-end world to bring the concept to life.
