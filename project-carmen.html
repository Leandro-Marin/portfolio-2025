CARMEN®

2023

CARMEN® is a brand design project for a Spanish-inspired café in Manchester, specializing in coffee and tortilla bocadillos. The goal was to create a strong and memorable visual identity that captures the essence of Spanish culture with a modern twist. From the logo to branded elements like cups, chairs, and packaging, every detail was designed to build a cohesive and recognizable brand. The project also extended to social media assets, ensuring a consistent and engaging presence across all platforms.
