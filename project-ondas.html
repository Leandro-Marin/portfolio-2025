PELUQUERIA ONDAS

2020

Ondas Peluquería is a Spanish hair salon that, with its new location, needed a fresh identity to match its transformation. The goal was to create a modern and stylish rebranding that reflected the salon's personality and expertise. The project began with the design of a dynamic new logo, capturing the essence of movement and creativity. From there, a full brand identity was developed, including color palettes, typography, product packaging, and promotional materials. The result is a cohesive and contemporary visual language that elevates the salon's image while maintaining its warmth and accessibility.
