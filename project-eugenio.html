EUGENIO

2021

Eugenio is an intelligent shopping assistant developed for Basko, a Genoese supermarket chain. Through a simple questionnaire, the algorithm analyzes the user's lifestyle and automatically selects the best-suited products, delivering them on a scheduled basis. My role in this project, developed at App To You, focused on UX design—creating wireframes for different screens and adapting the interface to Basko's corporate visual identity. Additionally, I contributed to the launch campaign, ensuring a seamless introduction of the service to customers through engaging visuals and clear communication.
