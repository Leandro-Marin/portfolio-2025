eugenio

2021

Eugenio is the new way to shop online with Basko, the Genoese supermarket chain. Its operation is easy. After registering and filling out a small questionnaire, Eugenio chooses the products that best suit the user’s lifestyle by bringing the groceries home every week. Project realized in agency App To You.
